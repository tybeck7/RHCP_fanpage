BIO

The Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American rock band from the 90's formed in Los Angeles. With a well know band consisting of Anthony Kiedis (singer), Flea(bassist),Chad Smith (drummer), and John Frusciante (guitarist). The band has sold over 100 million records worldwide making them one of the best selling artists of all time also putting them in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
